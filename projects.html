Powering Up the EU

A Tableau Infographic on the Primary Energy Production in the EU

Tableau
Description:

As part of the European Green Deal and the REPowerEU initiative, the EU not only aims to increase imports of clean energy but also raise primary production from various renewable sources, with the objective of achieving climate neutrality by 2050.

I created an infographic focusing on energy production in the EU that allows for the observation of energy production trends in the last 20 years and provides a map of the main power plants in Europe, along with their type and production capacity.

Retailer Sell-out

A Power BI report using various KPIs to compare the performance of two retailers in the video surveillance industry

Github
Description:

This Power BI report provides a comparative analysis of the performance of two retailers of a major player in the video surveillance industry. It focuses on their top 10 products, offering insights into sales trends and market dynamics. The data used in this report has been meticulously sanitized using standard anonymization techniques to comply with data protection regulations, making it impossible to link the data back to individuals or organizations.

A junction table was used to establish a relationship between each retailer's datasets (star schema). The report leverages data visualization techniques to present complex data in an easily understandable format, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions.

Sales Report (Pharmaceutical Company)

A multi-paged Power BI report comparing the performance of a company across five different countries

Github
Description:

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of sample data of a multinational pharmaceutical company's performance over a year across five European countries: the UK, Netherlands, Belgium, France, and Germany. The study is encapsulated in a multi-page Power BI report, meticulously designed to provide insights into the company's operations through KPIs.

The first page serves as an executive summary, providing a snapshot of the company's product sales and profit. It also includes a comparative analysis of actual sales versus targets for each product to gauge the company's performance against its strategic and operational goals.

The second page delves deeper into the company's operations across different countries. It provides visualizations that allow for an easy comparison of performance metrics across these markets. This detailed breakdown helps in identifying market-specific trends and opportunities.

Demo-Stat

A platform to navigate, visualize, and download Italian demographic indicators at the municipal, regional, and national level.

Online App
Description:

The Italian National Insitute of Statistics publishes demographic indicators at the national and regional level every year. However, it releases only raw demographic data for municipalities. Demo-Stat, an app developed in R and R Shiny, fills this gap by providing an interactive platform for exploring, downloading, visualizing, and interacting with demographic indicators for more than 7900 municipalities over the span of 20 years.

The application requests raw data from the National Institute of Statistics using istatAPI once a year. Then, the data is re-elaborated into five essential demographic indicators typically used in Research, which are then shown to the user in two distinct ways. The “Dashboard” view allows users to select a single municipality and observe all relevant indicators over a timespan of their choice. The “Plot & Compare” view enables users to compare how multiple municipalities perform on a single metric.

EU Inflation

A series of visualizations to observe Inflation in light of the recent inflation surge

Animation Animation Tutorial
Description:

This project offers a comprehensive overview of inflation trends in Europe, focusing on the major causes of the most recent inflationary surge. The project employs a key indicator devised by Eurostat, the Harmonised Index of Consumer Prices (HICP), which allows for the observation of overall inflation in the EU-27.

Fitbit Case Study

A study on the health and behavior of Fitbit users

Case Study
Description:

In this case study, I used data from 31 Fitbit users to uncover insights and patterns related to the behavior and health of Fitbit users in order to provide high-level recommendations to inform Fitbit's marketing strategy and improve the Fitbit app.

Cyclistic Case Study

A study on the behavior of different types of bike-sharing users

Case Study
Description:

This case study aims to delve into the usage patterns of Divvy ("Cyclistic" in the study), a bike-share company based in Chicago. The analysis provides a comprehensive understanding of how Casual Riders and Members use Divvy's bike-sharing services differently. The objective is to generate data-driven insights to provide high-level recommendations and inform a new marketing strategy aimed at increasing the conversion rate of casual riders into members.

According to the findings of the Exploratory Data Analysis, a vast amount of Casual Riders rely on Divvy's services from Friday to Sunday, while Members use Divvy's bikes consistently throughout the week. Consequently, a possible course of action would be to create a new, intermediate form of membership limited to the weekends, as it would be appealing to a vast quantity of casual riders and, at the same time, unappealing to current members.
